What is Volume?

Volume is the amount of space that an object or substance occupies. It is commonly measured in liters, milliliters, cubic meters, or gallons. For example, a water bottle may hold 1 liter of water, which is its volume.

✅ Advantages of Volume Measurement:
  • Helps in measuring liquids and gases accurately.
  • Useful in cooking, medical dosages, and scientific experiments.
  • Essential for industries like construction, shipping, and packaging.
  • Enables conversion between different units (liters, gallons, etc.).
❌ Disadvantages of Volume Measurement:
  • Not suitable for irregular-shaped objects without special methods.
  • Temperature and pressure can affect gas volume.
  • May require conversion tables for different unit systems (US vs UK gallons).
  • Measurement errors can occur without proper instruments.
