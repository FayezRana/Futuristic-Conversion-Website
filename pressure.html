What is Pressure?

Pressure is defined as the force applied per unit area. It tells us how much force is acting on a surface in a given area. The SI unit of pressure is Pascal (Pa).

Usage of Pressure:
  • Measuring tire pressure in vehicles
  • Monitoring blood pressure in healthcare
  • Industrial processes like gas storage & pipelines
  • Weather forecasting (air pressure)
